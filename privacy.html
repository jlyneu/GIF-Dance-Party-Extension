GIF Dance Party Extension Privacy Policy

This page is used to inform users of the GIF Dance Party Extension regarding policies involving the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information during the use of the GIF Dance Party Extension. If you choose to use the GIF Dance Party Extension, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. Any information collected is used for providing and improving the service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

The GIF Dance Party Extension does not collect, retain, or share any personally identifiable information or any contents of the websites where the service is being used. The service may modify the website where the service is being used in order to overlay the GIFs and GIF Dance Party Extension UI elements. Additionally, the service may temporarily add animations to the elements of the website where the service is being used. GIF search queries within the GIF Dance Party Extension are sent to the Giphy search API as necessary but are not stored nor shared with any other parties.

Data Storage

The GIF Dance Party Extension stores any custom GIF or song that you provide to the service in Chrome using the chrome.storage API. This information is stored to enhance the service and is not shared with any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
